Basic Artillery¶

I’ve always found it a bit odd that basic artillery trailers were not more common in BattleTech. I’ve created basic Age of War and later era towed artillery units based upon the Early Republic era Gun Trailer (Thumper).

Thumper, Simple: no engine, no turret
Thumper, Turret: engine and turret
Sniper, Simple: no engine, no turret
Sniper, Turret: engine and turret
Long Tom, Simple: no engine, no turret
Long Tom, Turret: engine and turret
